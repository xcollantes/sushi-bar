Company Information

Welcome to Tsukushinbo Traditional Japanese Cusine! You will not find another Japanese restaurant like this in Seattle! We specialize in making our sushi rolls with local fresh ingredients. All of vegatables are grown locally. And all of our fish is flown in daily. We also offer omakase, which means the chef will prepare something of his choice special for you. Because we offer such a wide variety of fresh fish and fresh vegatables our skillful can make something unique to you. If you love Japanese food, you have come to the right place. Our fantastic selections of entrees, sushi, sashimi , and sake will make you fall in love with Japanese food and drinks. We invite you to experience a warm, relaxing ambiance. You would love it. We will also help guide you through your first visit with a passion for our food unlike anywhere else.
