Company Information

Welcome to Tsukushinbo Traditional Japanese Cuisine! You will not find another Japanese restaurant like this in Seattle. We specialize in making our sushi rolls with local fresh ingredients. All of our vegetables are locally grown and all of our fish is flown in daily. We also offer omakase, which means the chef will prepare something of his special choice for you! Because we offer such a wide variety of fresh fish and fresh vegetables, our skillful chef can always make something unique. If you love Japanese food, you have come to the right place! Our fantastic selections of entrees, sushi, sashimi, and sake will make you fall in love with Japanese food and drinks. We invite you to experience a warm, relaxing ambience. We will help guide you through your first visit with a passion for our food unlike anywhere else.
